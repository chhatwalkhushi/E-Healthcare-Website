Coronavirus Prevention Do’s:-

Wash your hands frequently for 20+ seconds with soap and water

Stay at home and limit contact with others

Routinely clean frequently touched spaces in your home

Seek medical help if you’re suffering from a fever,cough or breathing

Limit contact with pets and animals



Stay clean,stay safe, wash your hands, avoid touching your face

Coronavirus Prevention Dont’s:-

Don’t go to crowded areas

Don’t touch your face

Don’t be in close contact with others

Don’t ignore symptoms

Don’t leave your home, especially if sick


Don't forget to download Aarogya Setu App and take a Self-Test



Who is at higher risk ?


Early information out of China, where Covid-19 first started, shows that some people are at higher risk of getting very sick from this illness. This includes:

  • Older Adults(Above 60).
  • People who smoke and drink or do both.
  • Those undergoing or going to get treatments like chemotherapy , dialysis, organ transplant surgeries
  • People who have serious chronic conditions like:
    • Heart Disease
    • Diabetes
    • Lung Disease
